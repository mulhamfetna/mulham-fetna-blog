## The mission

I work at the intersection of things that are usually kept apart: **mechatronics, data science, MLOps, research, STEM training, and open source.**

Syria's engineering talent is not the bottleneck — access is. Access to a research culture, to global open-source communities, to the tools and the credentials that turn ability into opportunity. Everything I build is aimed at that gap, and at making the path I had to improvise into one that others can simply follow.

**Reach:** Syria → the Arab world → globally.
