# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Mulham Fetna Blog** collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit this website or contact me for services.

## 1. Who controls your data

- **Controller:** Mulham Fetna
- **Contact email:** [mulham.fetna.blog@gmail.com](mailto:mulham.fetna.blog@gmail.com)

## 2. Information collected

I may collect:

1. **Contact information** you send voluntarily (such as name, email address, and message content).
2. **Usage and device data** through analytics tools, such as pages visited, approximate location, browser/device type, and referring source.
3. **Transactional information** needed to provide mentorship services (for example session type, scheduling details, and service-related communication records).

## 3. Cookies and analytics

This website uses **Google Analytics**. Google Analytics may set cookies and process usage data to help measure traffic and improve site performance.

You can control cookies through your browser settings and may block or delete cookies at any time. Blocking cookies may affect some site functionality.

## 4. Why data is used

Data is used to:

1. Respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
2. Manage mentorship communication and scheduling.
3. Improve website content, structure, and user experience.
4. Maintain site security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Legal basis (international/general approach)

Where applicable, processing is based on one or more of:

1. **Consent** (for voluntary submissions).
2. **Contractual necessity** (to deliver requested services).
3. **Legitimate interests** (site operations, analytics, and security).
4. **Legal compliance** (when required by law).

## 6. Data sharing

I do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers needed to operate the site and services, such as:

1. Website hosting/deployment providers.
2. Analytics provider (Google Analytics).
3. Communication and scheduling tools used to deliver services.

These providers process data under their own policies and applicable law.

## 7. Data retention

Personal data is retained only as long as needed for legitimate business, service, or legal purposes. When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymized where feasible.

## 8. International transfers

Because web infrastructure and providers may operate globally, your data may be processed outside your country. Reasonable safeguards are applied through reputable providers and standard contractual controls where available.

## 9. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may request to:

1. Access personal data.
2. Correct inaccurate data.
3. Delete personal data.
4. Restrict or object to processing.
5. Receive a copy of your data (data portability).
6. Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To submit a request, email: [mulham.fetna.blog@gmail.com](mailto:mulham.fetna.blog@gmail.com)

This site is not directed to children under 13. For users under 18 seeking services, parent/guardian consent may be required.

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect personal information. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## 12. Third-party links

This site may link to third-party websites. I am not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before submitting personal information.

## 13. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
